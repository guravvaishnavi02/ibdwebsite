Helping IBD Patients Across India

IBD Foundation is the one stop support for IBD patients across India, connecting them with self help resources, IBD guide and doctors.

Our Mission

IBD Patients across India deal with a loss of job, mental health troubles, chronic illness, expensive treatment, and lack of support issues. Our major aim is to bring the stakeholders and the patients in IBD journey closer to each other.

We are here

Through our multipronged approach in IBD Self Help and IBD Care, we help better.
