Who are we?

IBD Patient Support Foundation (India) is India's first and only non-profit working towards the welfare of persons with Inflammatory Bowel Disease in India. It is an organization that is by patients, for the patients. We are a grassroots organization working in close collaboration with some of the leading IBD centers in India.

Our Vision

Improve quality of life of people living with Inflammatory
Bowel Diseases in India, and ultimately help catalyse a cure for it.

Our Mission

  • Be strong advocates for the IBD community.
  • Remove the stigma surrounding IBD.
  • Bring doctors and patients together.
  • Strive for accessible, affordable healthcare.
  • Push for change via government policies.
  • Aid indigenous research efforts.
